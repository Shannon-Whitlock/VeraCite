VeraCite

A deterministic check for hallucinated and mangled citations. Paste a BibTeX bibliography and VeraCite verifies each entry against the authoritative record (Crossref, arXiv, OpenAlex).

Demo limits: up to 10 entries, verified against Crossref and arXiv (the AI relevance sweep is off, and the slower cross-source/ISBN checks the full tool runs are skipped here for speed). A run takes a few seconds. Nothing you paste is stored.

VeraCite never edits your bibliography — it only flags issues with the offending line and, where possible, a suggested fix. Source on GitHub.
